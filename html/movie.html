Top Movies of All Time

My best 3 movies.

Inside Out

"Inside Out" is a beautifully animated film that explores the emotions of an 11-year-old girl named Riley. It follows five core emotions—Joy, Sadness, Fear, Anger, and Disgust—as they navigate her move to a new city. With its vibrant animation, excellent voice acting, and insightful portrayal of emotions, the film is both entertaining and thought-provoking, appealing to audiences of all ages.

Perks of being a Wallflower

"The Perks of Being a Wallflower" is a touching coming-of-age novel told through letters written by Charlie, a shy and introspective high school freshman. The story explores themes of identity, friendship, and trauma as Charlie forms deep connections with seniors Sam and Patrick. It captures the raw emotions of adolescence, making the novel a powerful and relatable read about finding one's place in the world.

Better Days

"Better Days" is a powerful Chinese drama directed by Derek Tsang. It follows Chen Nian, a bullied student, and Bei, a small-time thug, as they navigate the harsh realities of high school and societal pressures. The film is visually captivating and deeply emotional, highlighting issues like bullying and mental health. Zhou Dongyu's performance is exceptional, making this a must-watch for its poignant storytelling and impactful themes.
